# «Hug» — Azanov Fleet

Hug is a purpose-built dive boat — small, nimble, equipped with a compressor and full gear. Ko Racha Yai, Ko Racha Noi — dive sites with visibility up to 20 metres. No tourist groups onboard — just your party and the underwater world of the Andaman.

Up to 10 guests. An experienced dive guide builds the programme to match the group's level — beginners to advanced. Snorkelling is available for those who don't dive.

Compact and functional — exactly what a serious dive day calls for.
