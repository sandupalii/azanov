# «Lagoon 42 Cloud Dancer» — Azanov Fleet

## Sailing catamaran. 42 feet. Wind, silence, islands.

Lagoon 42 Cloud Dancer is a sailing catamaran that doesn't rush. This is the choice for those who want to feel a real sail: quiet movement under wind, minimal engine, maximum atmosphere. Four cabins, two wide decks, a broad stern platform.

Ko Kai, Ko He, Ko Racha — routes follow the wind and weather. Snorkelling, swimming, fishing at anchor — the day builds itself on the water, at your pace.

Sailing is a different tempo. If Beach Club Yona is a party, Cloud Dancer is a meditation with Andaman views.
